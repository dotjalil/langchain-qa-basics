We conduct regular focus groups with industry leaders to better understand existing and future market demand, and provide training to meet this demand. In addition, our yearly job fair provides us with updated insights of what's needed today and what's missing and expected for tomorrow.
The Information technology market is always shaped with the distributive and emerging technologies. Our programs always reflect the latest developments and the rapid technological progress in the global technology industry. We keep monitoring future trends and seek various approaches to avail it to our trainers, community and learners.
Collaboration with fellow developing countries aims to capitalize on the institute' s experience to develop a strong & competitive human infrastructure in partner countries. This is through the provision of professional ICT capacity development programs that contribute to the upskilling of citizens with a wide spectrum of ICT skills necessary for daily life, as well as, driving the creation of job opportunities and the enhancement of employment.
Our programs provide more than 32 ICT disciplines that cover a wide technological spectrum including for example but not limited to data science to game development and digital marketing,..etc
